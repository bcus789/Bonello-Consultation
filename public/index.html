Enrico Bonello

Tree-Health Consulting

For when you think your tree is sick, and you need an expert opinion


Professional Advice

I am a currently active University Professor at The Ohio State University with more than 30 years academic experience, conducting top level research on tree health, from molecules to ecosystems, at top research institutions around the world, including Oxford University and the University of California at Berkeley and Davis. I am an expert on emerging and re-emerging diseases of trees, and biological invasions of forests (both natural and urban) by alien (exotic) disease-causing agents. I have taught or co-taught University-level forest health courses for the past 20 years and have published over 90 original research papers in top peer-reviewed journals.

I can provide professional recommendations when an expert opinion is required regarding (but not limited to)
  • - The need to remove a tree

  • - The need for deep pruning and other arboricultural surgical measures

  • - The need for trunk injection treatments

  • - The need for soil amelioration through amendments like Deep root fertilization and the application of mycorrhizal and other “organic” products

Of note, I do not provide arboricultural services myself, only professional consultation services.

Current Focus

In the last few years I have also developed an intense interest in advocating for the fundamental role of tree resistance research and deployment for the rational management of invasive tree pathogens and pests and, as an important derivation, for a radical change in how government handles management of invasive tree pathogens and pests. This activity has resulted in the formulation of coherent plans for the end-to-end management of such scourges, from how to handle potential sources of pests abroad to the best approaches to restore affected ecosystems. Besides my role as a faculty in Plant Pathology, I am a member of the Center for Applied Plant Sciences, the Environmental Science Graduate Program, the Infectious Disease Institute, and the Sustainability Institute.

Service as an Expert Witness

I am available to serve as an expert witness on matters pertaining to tree diseases.
